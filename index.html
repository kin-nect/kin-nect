How we're built

Free at the front door. Sustained at the back.

KIN-NECT is built with community at its core and longevity in its bones. Two complementary structures — one rooted in community interest, one in commerce — working together to keep the platform free and thriving.

Grants. Brand partnerships. Premium listings. Realm commission. Events. Revenue that serves the community, not the other way around.

Community Arm

KIN-NECT CIC

Community Interest Company. Free access for all, grants eligible, governed by and for the community it serves.

Commercial Arm

Realm LTD

The marketplace. A curated space for aligned businesses, makers, and practitioners to reach the community. Led by Chai.

The journey

Building slowly, building right

2026

The Foundations

Notion MVP. Instagram presence. Newsletter launched. No one leaves their job or restructures their life. Collectively, ~5–10 hours per week. This year is about planting seeds with intention.

2027

First Growth

First grant secured. Realm soft launch. Collaborators begin to be compensated per project. The community starts to feel the shape of what we're building.

2028

Proper Structure

Real compensation. Full structure in place. KIN-NECT as a self-sustaining, community-owned platform — exactly what it was always meant to be.
