What's coming — Future Pillars

The Vision

KIN-NECT is not finished. It's growing. These are the programmes, platforms and spaces being built next.

Year 2-3
🎙 KIN-NECT Podcast
Audio extension of the platform. Conversations from the directory — practitioners, makers, community builders. Our editorial, in voice.
Year 2-3 · Research
🍄 Psychedelic Research Programme
Community-based participatory research into plant medicine and consciousness. Partnering with Imperial College, Beckley Foundation and MAPS. Funded through KIN-NECT CIC grants.
Year 2-3 · Research
🌹 Psychosexual Research Strand
Research into conscious sexuality and psychosexual wellbeing, for and by the community. Educational, therapeutic and culturally rooted.
Year 2-3 · Events
🌿 KIN-NECT Retreats
Conscious community retreats beginning in Jamaica. Plant medicine, ancestral wellness, cannabis-friendly spaces in legal destinations. Ticket income + grant-funded.
Year 3-4 · Social Impact
🌱 Youth Prevention & Reform Programme
Led by G. Working with young people at risk — arts, music, community, mentorship. Heavily grant-funded territory. KIN-NECT's most important social impact work.
Year 3-4 · Social Impact
🏠 Homelessness Initiative
Community-led support for people experiencing homelessness. Built alongside and partnered with frontline organisations like Brixton Soup Kitchen CIC.
Year 3-5 · Capital Grant
🏛 KIN-NECT Physical Space
A community-owned building. Ground floor: library, events, practitioners. First floor: operations hub. Second floor: residency rooms. Attached: subsidised community apartments. Asset-locked — belongs to the collective permanently.
