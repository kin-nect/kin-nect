06 — Research

Evidence for Change

Evidence is at the heart of KIN-NECT. We believe it comes in many forms — the lived experience of our community, ancestral knowledge passed down through generations, the voices of practitioners working at the frontline, and new research we help produce. It is essential that we understand the needs and experiences of the communities we work alongside so that the change we make is meaningful and wanted.

"We think that evidence comes in lots of forms and places — including the lived experience of our team and the people who work with us; the knowledge of the communities we meet; and new qualitative and quantitative research we help produce."

Divine Play

Sexual health, intimacy and conscious relating — approached with the same care, rigour and cultural rootedness as everything else on this platform. Not titillation. Not shame. Just honesty about a fundamental part of human experience that our community deserves space to explore.

✦ The Model

Vendors keep 85% of every sale. KIN-NECT takes 15%. No upfront fees. No listing costs. You only pay when you sell. Event organisers keep 90%. KIN-NECT takes 10% per ticket. The community always accesses everything for free.

This is not a marketplace that extracts from the community. It is a marketplace that circulates within it.
